Mental Health Counceling

This service provides support for students dealing with stress, anxiety, depression, trauma, and other mental health challenges. It involves one-on-one sessions with trained counselors or therapists who offer guidance, coping strategies, and support tailored to the student's specific needs.

Group Health Counceling

Group sessions can be incredibly beneficial as they create a supportive environment where students facing similar challenges can share experiences, learn from each other, and develop coping skills under the guidance of a counselor or facilitator. It can cover various topics like stress management, social skills, or specific issues affecting a group of students.
