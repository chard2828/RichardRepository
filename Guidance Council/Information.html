About Us

Welcome to our guidance council! We are here to support and assist you on your academic and personal journey.

Our services include counseling, academic advising, career guidance, and more.

A Guidance Council, typically referred to as a Guidance Counsel, is a professional within the education system responsible for providing guidance and support to students, helping them navigate academic, personal, social, and career-related challenges. These professionals often work in schools or educational institutions, offering counseling, advising, and mentoring services to students.

Their roles may include:

Academic Guidance: Assisting students in choosing appropriate courses, addressing academic challenges, and helping them achieve their educational goals.

Personal and Emotional Support: Providing counseling and support for personal issues, mental health concerns, social interactions, and emotional well-being.

Career Counseling: Helping students explore career options, providing information about different professions, guiding them in career planning, and offering resources for job searches.

Conflict Resolution: Assisting in resolving conflicts or issues that students may face within the school environment, whether with peers, teachers, or parents.

Guidance counselors aim to facilitate the overall development of students by addressing various aspects of their lives, supporting them in making informed decisions, and fostering their personal growth and success.
